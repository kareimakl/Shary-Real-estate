About Ora

The Ark Development built Rafts 6 October Compound to offer its clients an elevated serene living experience in the West of Cairo. The project is a bewitching natural Eden, composed of various green and blue shades, harmoniously blending with the brilliant architecture in the compound.

Rafts Compound in the 6th of October City boasts a luxurious collection of units with a modern aesthetic, surrounded by lush open spaces from every corner. Additionally, the project exhibits a broad range of elite amenities that are aimed at granting homeowners a perfectly facilitated lifestyle.


The residents will have a hassle-free experience outside Rafts Compound as well, thanks to its strategic location in 6 of October City, near New Zayed, and specifically on Waslet Dahshour. It is near many popular destinations, vital facilities, and several main roads in the area, offering you a seamless commute to anywhere you want to reach.


The Master Plan of Rafts 6 October Compound
The Rafts 6 October Compound is a luxurious development where residents can step into a serene living experience with the comfort urban lifestyle brings. It is a unique vision in the heart of the West of Cairo, inspired by the psychological effects of blending both blue and green colors.

The master plan of Rafts Compound in 6 of October is well designed to outline its distinctive features. It seamlessly integrates the natural flow of sparking water, sketching a dynamic and dazzling landscape that brings a calming sensation to the hearts and minds of homeowners.

Rafts 6 October Compound features vast greenery and lush open spaces that enchantingly intertwine with artificial lakes and rivers, portraying a tranquil living environment like no other. The developer aimed to encourage the residents to start leading a healthy lifestyle that includes both their bodies and minds.

To motivate them to kickstart their health journey, The Ark included jogging and cycling tracks in the Rafts 6 October Compound, where they can have a rejuvenating morning stroll or a short night trip on their bicycle to destress.

Additionally, homeowners can bond with their families and loved ones amid the natural haven of The Rafts 6 of October Compound and have a blissful picnic. Not to mention that the project exhibits many mixed-use areas, filled with a myriad of recreational facilities, from retail outlets to fine dining options, where residents can have a delightful hangout a few minutes away from their homes.

Furthermore, to grant homeowners a safe and private experience, The Ark implemented unparalleled security measures and surveillance cameras that operate 24/7 in The Rafts Compound in the 6th of October City.


The Prime Location of Rafts Compound in 6 of October City
The Ark smartly picked the location of Rafts Compound in 6 October, close to New Zayed. The project is on Waslet Dahshour, Zone A, and in proximity to several main roads, such as:


  • The 26th of July Corridor
  • Boulevard Road


Additionally, the Rafts 6 of October Compound is near Al Motamayez District and West Somid. Not to mention that the project is minutes away from the distinctive facilities of bot and the 6th of October City, including:


  • Arkan Plaza
  • Nile University
  • Dar al Fouad Hospital
  • Mall of Arabia
  • Galleria 40



Property Types of Rafts Compound 6 October
Rafts 6 October Compound is the home of a lavish set of units, with various contemporary elegant designs, prices, areas, and features. The Ark Development made sure every family and individual preferences were met in the project.

There are luxurious 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments for sale in Rafts Compound in 6 October, as well as high-end studios and spacious villas and palaces. Not to mention that the project is uniquely crafted by Raef Fahmy, one of the leading architects who ensures unparalleled elegance and timeless designs.


The Advantages of Living in Rafts 6 October and the Unique Selling Points of the Compound
Your next chapter in life will be with a unique address when you own your unit in Rafts 6 October, as the location of the compound was ideally chosen on Waslet Dahshour Road, one of the most elite living destinations in the area. It is surrounded by many restaurants, cafes, shopping venues, and leisure facilities. Additionally, the project is accessible via many main roads, ensuring you can easily commute to anywhere in Cairo.

Your lifestyle in Rafts October Compound will be distinctive as well, thanks to the matchless amenities The Ark included in the project. It didn't only develop a state-of-the-art residential project but also somewhere the residents can have a blissful living experience where their human connections are nourished.

Furthermore, your home in The Rafts of Ark 6 October Compound would depict elegance at its finest, designed by Raef Fahmy. It would be surrounded by greenery and glistening water features that diffuse their harmonious vibes throughout your windows.
